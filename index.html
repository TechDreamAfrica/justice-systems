About

Building the fairer future of work.

Justice Systems was founded on a simple belief: AI should make work more human, not less.

Our mission

To democratize AI for every team — so small startups can compete with enterprises, and every worker can focus on what matters most.

Our vision

A world where AI handles the repetitive, the tedious, and the time-consuming — freeing humans to create, connect, and think deeply.

Our story

Justice Systems began in 2024 in a small San Francisco apartment. Our founders — engineers and operators who'd spent years watching teams drown in repetitive work — set out to build the AI platform they wished existed.

We saw teams paying for six different tools to handle support, marketing, and code. We saw small businesses priced out of enterprise AI. We saw developers staring at blank editors at 2am.

So we built one platform that does all three — beautifully, securely, and affordably. Today, 12,000+ teams trust Justice Systems to power their work. We're just getting started.
